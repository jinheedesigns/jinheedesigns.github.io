Background

A little bit about me and what I do!

Having experience as both an in-house and freelance designer, I've worked on a variety of design projects. On the side, I also dabble in motion graphics and animation, so I wear many hats in the design world.

  • Visual Marketing: social media graphics, reels, email campaigns
